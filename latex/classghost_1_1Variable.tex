\subsection{Detailed Description}
\hyperlink{classghost_1_1Variable}{Variable} is the class encoding the variables of your C\-S\-P/\-C\-O\-P. 

In G\-H\-O\-S\-T, all variable objects must be instanciate from the same concrete class. Be careful to model your C\-S\-P/\-C\-O\-P in order to use one kind of variable only.

To encode your C\-S\-P/\-C\-O\-P variables, you can either directly use this class \hyperlink{classghost_1_1Variable}{Variable} (there are no pure virtual functions here), or inherit from it to make your own variable class.
